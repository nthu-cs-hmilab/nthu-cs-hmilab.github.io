About
HMI LAB

Human-Centered Machine Intelligence Lab was established on July 1, 2020 at Department of Computer Science, National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan.

Our research focuses on both decoding the human intelligence through neuroimaging and adopting machine intelligence to solve human needs.

We develop technology that reflects human intelligence and is explainable to address the AI black-box problem. Our goal is to build AI systems to enhance and support humans rather than replace them.
